Privacy Policy

Personal data, or personal information, means any information about an individual from which that person can be identified. It does not include data where the identity has been removed (anonymous data). We do not store any your personal information such as email address. We have nowhere to store it. We don’t even have a server database to store it for you to use our service.
This page is used to inform you regarding our policies with the use of your information if you decided to use our Service.
Our service uses the Google Data API(s) which follow Google's privacy policy and Google Term of Service.

What information we use

Our service can access your google accounts to use Google APIs.
We request you to sign-in with google so that our service get authorization from google to use Google API. Of course, you can remove account access of our service. If so, you may not access or use our service.
Our service is in compliance with YouTube Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy.

How and Why we use information

We use account information you enter in our service for the purposes listed below:
- To allow our service to access google APIs
- To let you collect videos and comments about a specific issue using google APIs

Usage of google APIs

Our service use google APIs(Google Data API v3 https://developers.google.com/youtube/v3?hl) for the purposes listed below:
- To enable users to collect videos and comments on YouTube. For example, users can collect videos about ‘Election’.
- To enable users to analyze the collected data. For example, users can find most popular video referring to 'like' for the video which has been collected using Google APIs.
- To enable users to store the result of analysis on user’s device.
Our service gathers the following data from YouTube.
- Video: Video ID, Title, Created Time, Writer ID, Writer Name, View count, Like and Dislike count, Comment count, Description
- Comment: Comment ID, Created Time, Writer ID, Writer Name, Like count, Reply count, Video ID, Full text
